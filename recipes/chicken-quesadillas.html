Chicken Quesadillas

Description

Chicken quesadillas are a delicious and versatile dish that combines tender chicken, melted cheese, and flavorful spices folded into a warm tortilla. To prepare this savory delight, begin by cooking diced chicken breast in a skillet with olive oil until golden brown and fully cooked. Season the chicken generously with a blend of chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and salt to infuse each bite with a zesty kick. While the chicken simmers to perfection, warm a large tortilla on a separate skillet until it becomes pliable and slightly toasted. Next, spread a layer of shredded Monterey Jack and cheddar cheese over one half of the tortilla, allowing the cheese to melt and create a gooey base. Once the cheese begins to bubble, add the seasoned chicken evenly atop the melted cheese. Fold the tortilla in half to encapsulate the filling, pressing down gently to ensure a crisp exterior and creamy interior.

For an extra burst of flavor, serve these chicken quesadillas with sides such as homemade guacamole, salsa fresca, or sour cream for dipping. The combination of tender chicken, melted cheese, and perfectly toasted tortillas makes these quesadillas a crowd-pleasing favorite for any occasion. Whether served as a quick weeknight dinner or as a festive appetizer at gatherings, these quesadillas are sure to impress with their savory blend of spices and comforting warmth.

Ingredients

  • 6 Tbsp. vegetable oil, divided
  • 2 bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 1/2 tsp. chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp. dried oregano
  • 4 medium flour tortillas
  • 2 c. shredded Monterey jack
  • 2 c. shredded cheddar
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  • Sour cream, for serving

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat 1 tbsp. neutral oil. Add peppers and onion and season with salt. Cook until soft, 5 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
  2. Add 1 tbsp. neutral oil to pan and heat over medium-high heat. Add chicken to pan and season with chili powder, cumin, dried oregano, and ½ teaspoon salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until golden and cooked through, 8 minutes. Transfer to a plate. Wipe out skillet.
  3. Add 1 tbsp. neutral in skillet. Add 1 flour tortilla to skillet and top half of the tortilla with a heavy sprinkling of both cheeses, ¼ of the cooked chicken mixture, ¼ of the pepper-onion mixture, a few slices of avocado, and a sprinkling of scallions. Fold the other half of the tortilla over and cook, flipping once, until golden, 3 minutes per side.
  4. Repeat with remaining oil, tortillas, chicken, pepper-onion mixture, avocado, and scallions to make 4 quesadillas.
  5. Slice into wedges and serve with sour cream.
